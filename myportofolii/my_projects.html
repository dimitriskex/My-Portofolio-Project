Ethical AI Frameworks for Gender Bias

Nov 2025 – Apr 2026


This project was developed as part of my undergraduate thesis under the supervision of Professor Fotini Paraskeva at the University of Piraeus. It focuses on “Ethical AI Frameworks for Gender Bias,” exploring ethical principles, international frameworks, and guidelines for the responsible development of AI systems. As AI continues to play an increasingly significant role in decision-making, ensuring fairness and equality has become more important than ever.
